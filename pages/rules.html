Platform Rules

Please follow these rules to keep APPL-AI safe, professional, and fair for everyone.

01

Use real and accurate information

Users must provide truthful profile details, job descriptions, and application information.

02

Respect other users

Harassment, discrimination, abusive language, and inappropriate behavior are not allowed.

03

No fake job postings

Employers must only post legitimate job opportunities and must not mislead applicants.

04

No spam or misuse

Users must not spam applications, duplicate posts excessively, or misuse system features.

05

Protect privacy

Do not share sensitive personal data of other users without consent.

06

Follow fair hiring practices

Employers should review applicants fairly and avoid biased or harmful screening behavior.
