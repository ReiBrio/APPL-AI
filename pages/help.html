Help Center

Find answers to common questions and learn how to use APPL-AI properly.

How do I apply for a job?

Open a job post from the Home page, click the Apply button, then upload your resume and submit your application.

How do I save a job?

Click the save icon on a job card. Saved jobs will appear in your Saved page for easier access later.

How do employers review applicants?

Employers can open their profile, select a job post, and use the View Applicants page to review applications and update statuses.

What do notifications mean?

Notifications inform you about important actions, such as new applications, approved applications, rejected applications, and other job-related updates.

Why can’t I see some buttons?

Some buttons are role-based. Applicants can apply and save jobs, while employers can create posts and manage applicants.

What should I do if something is not loading?

Refresh the page, make sure your internet is stable, and check whether your session is still active. If the issue continues, log out and log back in.
